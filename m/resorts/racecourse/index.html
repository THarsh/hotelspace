AN 'ENDLESS SUMMER' STAY

Tucked inside Fig Tree Hall at the University of New South Wales, Racecourse Space is under ten minutes away from legendary surf break at world famous Coogee Beach. Almost everything is within walking distance, but public transport is also available to provide easy access to the airport and Sydney’s most popular events and attractions.

Other highlights of the neighborhood include the ever popular Ritz Cinema, the essential Royal Randwick Racecourse, a vibrant nightlife scene and some of the best Asian restaurants in all of Sydney.
