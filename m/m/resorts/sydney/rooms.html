This summer, kiss those couchsurfing blues goodbye and live like a local with a stay at Free Space. Reserve a stylish room at one of our four popup spots around Sydney and enjoy a repurposed space that offers affordable accommodations without compromise.  

Whether you want to hang ten at Coogee Beach or take ten exploring niche neighborhood haunts, our boho chic retreat is an effortless way to stay close to all the action. Coogee, Sydney

Laidback surf culture thrives at the very core of Coogee, a seaside suburb just outside of Sydney. Coogee Beach and the Tasman Sea lay towards the east, drawing in year round crowds for swimming, surfing and sunbathing along the scenic coast.
