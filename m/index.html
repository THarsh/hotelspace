Glebe, Sydney

Lush green streets and historic architecture set the stage for the sprawling suburb of Glebe. Bohemian chic meets modern academia and the bustling streets - lined with cafes, bookstores and boutiques - consistently draw in a creative crowd.

Coogee, Sydney

Laidback surf culture thrives at the very core of Coogee, a seaside suburb just outside of Sydney. Coogee Beach and the Tasman Sea lay towards the east, drawing in year round crowds for swimming, surfing and sunbathing along the scenic coast.
