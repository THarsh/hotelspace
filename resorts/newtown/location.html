Newtown's King Street And Beyond

Hands-down the most creative and inspiring of Sydney’s inner city suburbs, Newtown sparkles with non-stop vibrant activity. Trendy bars, quirky pubs, vintage stores, art and antique galleries, theatres, markets and fashion boutiques all spilling out from your hotel doorstep. An eclectic urban hub, Newtown offers endless opportunities to spark your inner creative genius.

No visit to Newtown is complete without time spent on bustling King Street. Spend the afternoon at a sidewalk café. Explore an urban park. Shop for antiques, art and vintage vinyl. Savor the traditional home cooking of Ralf’s Café, a university institution.

Enmore Theatre

118-132 Enmore Rd, Newtown NSW 2042 | Tel. +61 2 9550 3666

Since first opening in 1908, the Enmore Theatre remains the longest running live music venue in New South Wales. Aside from hosting stand up comedy, beauty pageants and children’s entertainment, the history and atmosphere of the building have also attracted some of the world's largest musicians to date: The Rolling Stones, KISS, Coldplay, Nas, Oasis, Paramore, Ween, The Offspring, Arctic Monkeys, The White Stripes, Kraftwerk, and Marilyn Manson - to name a few!

Earl's Juke Joint

407 King St, Newtown NSW 2042

Earls’ Juke Joint is a New Orleans inspired speakeasy with exposed brick walls, dark wood, and a corrugated metal ceiling. This neighborhood cocktail bar is tucked away inside an unassuming old butcher’s shop, complete with lace curtains, and spins swanky music that traces back through the evolution of black music.

University of Technology Sydney (UTS)

15 Broadway, Ultimo NSW 2007 | Tel. +61 2 9514 2000

UTS is one of the largest universities in Australia, offering undergraduate and postgraduate courses across traditional and emerging studies such as architecture, built environment, business, communication, design, education, engineering, information technology, international studies, law, midwifery, nursing, pharmacy and science.

Carriageworks

245 Wilson St, Eveleigh NSW 2015 | Tel. +61 2 8571 9099

Carriageworks is the largest and most significant contemporary arts centre of its kind in Australia, providing support and studio space to resident and international artists. Housed in the old Eveleigh Rail Yards, the distinctive 19th century industrial atmosphere of the former railway carriage and blacksmith workshops have been retained, making it a unique and inspiring environment in which to create and house artwork.

Paddy's Markets

Market City, Hay St & Thomas Street, Sydney NSW 2000 | Tel. +61 1300 361 589

Paddy’s Markets at Haymarket is a traditional flea market located beside Sydney’s Chinatown in the central business district. Specialising in imported clothes, giftware and souvenirs, (with a small produce section), it has become a popular one-stop-shop for locals and tourists alike.  

Broadway Shopping Centre

1 Bay St, Broadway NSW 2007 | Tel. +61 2 8398 5620

Located inside one of the two former Grace Bros Broadway buildings, this recently remodeled shopping centre features a 500-seat food court, Hoyts 12 Screen Cinema, over 100 specialty stores, and two supermarkets to meet all of your dining, shopping and entertainment needs.

Newtown Hotel

174 King Street Newtown, NSW 2042 | Tel. +61 2 9557 6399

The Apple Daily Bar & Eating House is a hip and swanky space inside the Newtown Hotel, serving up Asian-inspired fare, with a menu created to be shared. Between the crispy pork belly, steamed buns, papaya salad and pad thai, you’re bound to find something savory to sink your teeth into.

Cow and the Moon (gelato)

181 Enmore Rd, Enmore NSW 2042 | Tel. +61 2 9557 4255

The passionate crew at Cow and the Moon are all about authentically Italian gelato, artisanal treats and tasty fair trade coffee, all handcrafted with love of course. Nestled in the heart of Enmore, come explore their world of sweet temptations as you snack and sip your way through their shop.

Black Star Pastry

277 Australia St, Newtown NSW 2042 | Tel. +61 2 9557 8656

Owner and head chef Christopher Thé and his team work the old fashioned way - with love and integrity. Sourcing seasonal produce from fresh markets and steering away from fake flavors and dyes, they bring out the natural taste of their pretty pastries through creative techniques and time-honored traditions.

Young Henry's Brewery

76 Wilford Street Newtown NSW 2042 | Tel +61 2 9519 0048

A large industrial warehouse space with a state-of-the art distillery that specializes in high quality, hand crafted spirits using uniquely Australian ingredients and low impact brewing.
