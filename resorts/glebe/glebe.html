POP-UP PERFECTION

The green streets of Glebe are lined with bustling cafes, vintage bookstores and stylish boutiques. Lush landscapes and historic architecture set the scene, where boho chic meets modern academia in this ultra-hip suburb of Sydney.

Consistently drawing in a creative crowd, Glebe Space accommodations overlook quiet courtyards with blooming gardens, and out across the University of Sydney’s city skyline. Glebe Space offers modern guest rooms with ensuite amenities and contemporary decor, all situated inside the new Sancta Sophia Graduate House on campus.
