Our Property

A Perfect Home Base For Exploring Glebe

Located on the University of Sydney’s Sancta Sophia College campus, FreeSpace Glebe is tucked within a charming building on Missenden Road in Campertown. Guests enjoy single and shared rooms overlooking the tranquil college quad and gardens. A shared roof terrace offers beautiful views of Sydney’s skyline.

The hotel is a short walk from bohemian King Street, Newtown, one of Sydney’s live music hubs, where you’ll find dozens of laid-back restaurants and bars, quirky boutiques and bookstores, theatres and clubs.

Central Location

Located on the University of Sydney’s main campus, FreeSpace Glebe is located in close proximity to the city’s transportation hubs, major attractions and the central business district.

Friendly Atmosphere

Our knowledgeable, friendly staff is ready to help you to plan a memorable experience in and around Sydney. They’ll share their best insider tips for getting around, favorite attractions and off-the-beaten-path experiences.

Free WiFi

Our guests enjoy complimentary WiFi throughout the hotel when they book their reservation direct. Post your fave photos to Instagram, check-in with the office, research tomorrow’s day trip...all at high speed.

Rooftop Terrace

Our rooftop terrace is a great place to kick back after a day of exploring. Gather with new-found friends, sip a fine shiraz from the nearby Hunter Valley wine-making region and take in the city’s sparkling skyline views.

Rooms To Suit Your Style

Choose a shared room with convenient access to shared spaces for socializing, meal prep and communal dining or opt for the privacy of one of our new studios featuring en-suite bathrooms and kitchenettes.
