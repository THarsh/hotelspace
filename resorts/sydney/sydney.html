SUBURBAN STYLE, SYDNEY SPACE

Sydney Space is located at the University of Sydney’s St. John’s College in Camperdown. Stay within walking distance of the King Street shopping district and countless neighborhood hangs. Public transportation is readily available and large scale inner city attractions are only a hop, skip and a shake away.  

The modern guest rooms at Sydney Space are stylish and bright. Accommodations are available in a range of configurations, making Sydney Space the perfect pop-up spot to stay during your summer stay in Oz.
